A Day In New York

6 March, 2021 | Travelling

Today it was a good day in my favourite city ,New York. I went for a nice trip alone. Oh ,No I was not really alone. I had my amazing friend with me , my camera, We saw those cool high buildings and of course my camera will remeber them better than me because she has a photographic memory, I think. Of course we have also seen those people in a hurry trying to catch up with the city that never sleep.
